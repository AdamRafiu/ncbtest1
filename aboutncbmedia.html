About NCBMedia.com

The Term NCB MEDIA Founded in 2023, NCB Media, originally known as City Syd News was created to address the challenge of finding accurate and up-to-date information about musicians and dancers worldwide. Recognizing the need for a reliable source, NCB Media was established to provide the latest news, trends, and stories in the entertainment industry.

With its roots in Tamale and the Bono East Region of Ghana, NCB Media has evolved from a local initiative into a global platform. Today, NCB Media is the premier online destination for entertainment news, offering comprehensive coverage and insights into the vibrant world of music and dance.
